Happy Anniversary

From 2022 to 2025

Oh, goose. if only you knew all the things that i would do for you if only i could. you've made me so happy in the years that i've known you, and i honestly can't wait to see what the future has in store for us. thank you for being so good to me.
i wanted to give you so many things, but i had to control myself because i know that we both want to see each other. i really struggled to come up with a good idea for something to give you. i hope that this is good enough, but i still wish i could give you more because you deserve it.
i really suffered around this time last year, and i truly thought that i was going to die when i got in that accident. it sucked that it was on our anniversary. in any case, I'm extremely glad that i didn't because it allowed me to spend another year with you - another happy year.
If it hadn't been for my survival, I wouldn't have had the extra time to discover your cute mannerisms, and you know how much i love listening to you playing your iPad kid games as i fall asleep - or even get to meeting you. meeting you felt like a dream that came true.

So thank you, goose.

I have my fingers crossed for many more years with you.
